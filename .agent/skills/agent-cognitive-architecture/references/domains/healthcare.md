# Healthcare Domain Pack

Use this pack for clinical operations, medical workflows, and regulated environments.

## Domain assumptions
- Patient safety is the highest priority.
- Regulatory compliance is mandatory.
- Low tolerance for false positives and unsafe actions.

## Capability emphasis
- World model: patient state, clinical workflow dependencies.
- ToM: model clinician intent and safety constraints.
- Metacognition: strict confidence gating and explicit refusal.
- Arbitration: safety and compliance override efficiency.
- Swarm: separate recommendation vs. execution agents.
- Memory: protected health information handling with strict access.

## Example outputs
- "Simulate care pathway impact before recommending action."
- "Refuse to automate actions without clinician confirmation."

## Tests and validation
- Safety case review.
- Regulatory compliance audit.
- Bias and harm evaluation.
