# Enterprise IT Domain Pack

Use this pack for IT operations, service management, and enterprise automation.

## Domain assumptions
- SLA adherence and change windows are critical.
- Human-in-the-loop approvals are common.
- Stability and predictability outweigh novelty.

## Capability emphasis
- World model: service dependency graphs and change impact.
- ToM: stakeholder intent and business priority modeling.
- Metacognition: defer to human approvals on ambiguous changes.
- Arbitration: SLAs and compliance override optimization.
- Swarm: planner, change executor, and verifier separation.
- Memory: change history and rollback outcomes.

## Example outputs
- "Simulate outage risk before applying patch."
- "Escalate to human approver when confidence < 0.8."

## Tests and validation
- Change simulation and rollback drills.
- Policy compliance checks.
- Incident post-mortem consistency.
