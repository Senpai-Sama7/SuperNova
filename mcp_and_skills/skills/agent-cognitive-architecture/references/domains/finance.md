# Finance Domain Pack

Use this pack for trading systems, risk management, and compliance-heavy workflows.

## Domain assumptions
- Regulatory oversight and auditability are mandatory.
- Latency can matter, but safety and compliance dominate.
- Adversaries include market manipulation and fraud actors.

## Capability emphasis
- World model: market state, liquidity, counterparty risk.
- ToM: adversarial intent and manipulation detection.
- Metacognition: strict confidence thresholds, fail-safe modes.
- Arbitration: compliance and capital constraints override return.
- Swarm: separate risk, execution, and oversight agents.
- Memory: immutable audit logs and policy versioning.

## Example outputs
- "Simulate execution impact and slippage before trade placement."
- "Block actions that violate risk limits or regulatory constraints."

## Tests and validation
- Backtesting and stress testing.
- Compliance rule regression checks.
- Adversarial scenario simulation.
